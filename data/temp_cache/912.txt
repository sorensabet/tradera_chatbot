QUESTION: What types of purchases are prohibited by Tradera's Rules for Sellers?
ANSWER: Tradera prohibits the purchase of properties (including residential properties), customized objects (unless the recipient claims that the objects have not arrived), items equivalent to cash (including gift cards), tickets for events such as sports or concerts, and goods and services that are against the platform's rules for sellers.

QUESTION: How does Tradera's dispute settlement procedure work?
ANSWER: If there is any difference of opinion between a seller and a buyer, they can use Tradera's dispute settlement system offered on "My Tradera". The dispute settlement procedure must be initiated within thirty (30) days from the date of the purchase, and all communication about the issue must be made via Tradera's dispute settlement system. If the parties fail to resolve the issue within ten (10) days, the buyer has an option to escalate the issue to Tradera.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION: What happens if the buyer does not escalate the dispute to Tradera within ten days of the initiation of the dispute settlement procedure?
ANSWER: If the buyer does not escalate the dispute to Tradera within ten (10) days of initiating the dispute settlement procedure, the settlement will automatically be terminated in the Seller's favor.
