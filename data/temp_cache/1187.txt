QUESTION 1: What is Tradera's role in payments for transactions?
ANSWER: Tradera ensures payment to the seller, either for a separate transaction or as a combined amount involving several transactions, after the transaction has been approved by Tradera. However, the payment services themselves are not provided by Tradera, and irrespective of the payment method a Buyer chooses. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-1-3/

QUESTION 2: How are payment deductions calculated by Tradera?
ANSWER: Before payment is made, any amount that the Seller is obliged to pay to Tradera will be deducted by Tradera. The amounts that Tradera will deduct may relate to selling expenses, refundable amounts, and fees to Tradera agreed between the Seller and Tradera under separate agreement. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-1-3/

QUESTION 3: What happens if the Seller has not provided Tradera with accurate payment information?
ANSWER: If the Seller has not provided Tradera with accurate payment information, Tradera will not be able to pay the Seller. In such a case, Tradera will contact the Seller to request the outstanding information. Once Tradera has received the information required to make the payment, the Seller will generally be paid within two (2) business days. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-1-3/

QUESTION 4: When does payment from a buyer to a seller happen on Tradera?
ANSWER: Upon Tradera's confirmation that the Buyer has made a payment, the Seller shall immediately send the goods to the Buyer, as described in more detail below under the description of each payment method. Tradera reserves the right to withhold payment if and for as long as there is a suspicion that the Seller will not fulfill his/her obligations. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-1-3/

QUESTION 5: How do Swish payments work on Tradera?
ANSWER: When a Buyer wishes to pay by Swish, the payment goes directly from the Buyer's account to the Seller's account. Tradera's payment solution facilitates the transaction by automatically filling in the Seller's telephone number in the Swish app, and the Buyer can then authorize the payment directly. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-1-3/

QUESTION 6: What is required for a seller to get paid on Tradera?
ANSWER: The Seller needs to provide Tradera with accurate payment information to receive payment. If the Seller has not provided accurate payment information, Tradera will not be able to pay the Seller.
