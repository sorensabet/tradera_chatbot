QUESTION 1: What kind of services does Tradera provide?
ANSWER: Tradera provides location-based services such as advertising, search results, and other personalized content. They also use information shared by users to suggest connections between them and people they may know or want to transact with through the platform.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 2: What is the purpose of using user information to suggest connections on Tradera?
ANSWER: The purpose of using user information to suggest connections on Tradera is to make it easier for users to find and connect with others on the platform.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 3: Can Tradera contact me in response to a question submitted to their customer service team?
ANSWER: Yes, Tradera may contact a user in response to a question submitted to their customer service team.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 4: What is the reason for Tradera using personal data for analytics purposes?
ANSWER: Tradera may use personal data for analytics purposes to manage their everyday business needs or monitor the platform.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 5: Does Tradera sell or rent user personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes?
ANSWER: No, Tradera does not sell or rent user personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes without explicit consent from the user.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 6: How can a user review Tradera's User Cookie Notice?
ANSWER: A user can review Tradera's User Cookie Notice for more information on how to opt-out of third-party cookies for advertising.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 7: What happens if a user withdraws their consent for the use or disclosure of their Personal Data?
ANSWER: If a user withdraws their consent for the use or disclosure of their Personal Data, they may not be able to access Tradera's websites, applications, or tools or receive some or all of the services and customer support offered.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 8: Can a user indicate their communication preference with Tradera?
ANSWER: Yes, a user can indicate their communication preference within their My Tradera page or within a direct communication from Tradera.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 9: How does Tradera personalize their services?
ANSWER: Tradera personalizes their services by delivering targeted marketing and promotional offers based on a user's communication preferences and measuring and improving their advertising based on the user's ad customization preferences.

URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION 10: What is the guideline for obtaining user consent for marketing purposes?
ANSWER: Tradera obtains user consent for marketing purposes in a manner consistent with applicable regulations and their Privacy Statement.
