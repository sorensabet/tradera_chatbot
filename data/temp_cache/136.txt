QUESTION: What is Tradera's registration process?
ANSWER: To complete your registration at Tradera, more information is required to verify your account. This includes submitting an extract of the population register, which can be found on the tax ministries homepage. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/privatkonto-pa-tradera/

QUESTION: What is an extract from the population register?
ANSWER: An extract from the population register is a verification that you are a citizen. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/privatkonto-pa-tradera/

QUESTION: Where can I find an extract from the population register?
ANSWER: You can find an extract from the population register on the tax ministries homepage by clicking on "Print a proof of citizenship" and logging in with your electronic ID. Save the file as a PDF. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/privatkonto-pa-tradera/

QUESTION: Why do I have to submit an extract from the population register for verification on Tradera?
ANSWER: The information provided is not always sufficient to verify one's identity. Verification through the extract of the population register helps ensure a safe and secure marketplace for all members. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/privatkonto-pa-tradera/

QUESTION: Where do I send my proof of citizenship for verification on Tradera?
ANSWER: You can send your proof of citizenship to Tradera by clicking on the link provided on the website. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/privatkonto-pa-tradera/
