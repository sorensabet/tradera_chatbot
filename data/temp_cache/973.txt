QUESTION 1: What kind of personal data could be accessed by Tradera when users associate their account with a third party managed account?
ANSWER: The personal data that Tradera may access varies depending on the third party website, the user's privacy settings on that website and their consent.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/integritetespolicy-eng/

QUESTION 2: What is the purpose of collecting personal data according to the privacy statement?
ANSWER: The purpose of collecting personal data is to provide users with access to Tradera's websites and services, to provide requested customer service, to offer users a safe and customised experience, and to prevent and detect fraudulent or illegal activities.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/integritetespolicy-eng/

QUESTION 3: What can Tradera use the collected personal data for?
ANSWER: Tradera can use the collected personal data to manage user accounts, provide customer support, prevent and investigate fraudulent or illegal activities, customise services and improve advertising, contact users to resolve disputes, collect fees, and troubleshoot problems, verify information for accuracy, and provide other services requested by users.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/integritetespolicy-eng/

QUESTION 4: What legal obligation does Tradera have when processing personal data?
ANSWER: Tradera must have an applicable legal ground, and their processing of personal data is based on legal obligations.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/integritetespolicy-eng/

QUESTION 8: What is the user agreeing to when they associate their account managed by a third party with their Tradera account and grant Tradera access to their information?
