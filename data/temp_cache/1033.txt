QUESTION: What information is disclosed by Tradera in case of piracy or unlawful activity?
ANSWER: In case of piracy or unlawful activity, Tradera will disclose personal information such as name, personal identification number, street address, phone number, email address, and company name, as allowed by applicable laws.

QUESTION: What is a Tradera User ID?
ANSWER: A Tradera User ID is a unique identifier assigned to each user and it is displayed throughout Tradera. All of a user's Tradera activity is connected to their User ID, including bids, purchases, items for sale, storefronts, feedback, ratings, and associated comments.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION: What information is associated with a Tradera User ID?
ANSWER: Other users can see a user's bids, purchases, items for sale, storefronts, feedback, ratings, and associated comments. Any information a user chooses to share in their profile is also visible to other users.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/privacy-statement-2021-2023/

QUESTION: What happens if a user associates their name with their Tradera User ID?
ANSWER: If a user associates their name with their Tradera User ID, the people to whom they have revealed their name will be able to personally identify their Tradera activities.
