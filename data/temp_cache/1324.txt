QUESTION 1: What purchases are not covered by Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy?
\ANSWER: Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy does not cover purchases of vehicles (including boats), purchases of custom-made items (unless they are claimed to have not been received), purchases of items equivalent to cash (including gift cards), purchases of event tickets, and purchases of goods or services that are prohibited under Tradera's Rules for Sellers.

QUESTION 2: On what platform can disputes between buyers and sellers be resolved?
\ANSWER: Disputes between buyers and sellers on Tradera must be resolved using Tradera's dispute resolution system on the Tradera platform.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 3: When must a buyer open a dispute resolution process on Tradera after making a payment?
\ANSWER: A buyer must open a dispute resolution process on Tradera within 30 days of the date on which they made the payment.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 4: What happens if a buyer and seller cannot reach a solution to their problem within ten days?
\ANSWER: If a buyer and seller cannot reach a solution within ten days of the dispute resolution process being opened, the buyer can escalate the dispute to Tradera.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 5: What happens if a buyer does not escalate a dispute to Tradera within ten days?
\ANSWER: If a buyer does not escalate a dispute to Tradera within ten days of the dispute resolution process being opened, the process will automatically be closed in the seller's favour.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 6: What materials can Tradera ask both parties for when a dispute is escalated to them?
\ANSWER: When a dispute is escalated to Tradera, they can ask both the buyer and seller for supporting materials, such as further documents relating to the goods or the transaction.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 7: Are vehicles covered by Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy?
\ANSWER: Vehicles, including boats, are not covered by Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy, with the exception of personally portable light vehicles used for recreational purposes, such as bicycles and wheeled hoverboards.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 8: Can purchases of custom-made items be covered by Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy?
\ANSWER: Purchases of custom-made items are not covered by Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy, unless they are claimed to have not been received.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 9: Is there a time limit for the communication between the buyer and seller during the dispute resolution process?
\ANSWER: There is no time limit for the communication between the buyer and seller during the dispute resolution process, but if they cannot reach a solution within ten days, the buyer can escalate the dispute to Tradera.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION 10: Can purchases of goods or services that are prohibited under Tradera's Rules for Sellers be covered by Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy?
\ANSWER: Purchases of goods or services that are prohibited under Tradera's Rules for Sellers are not covered by Tradera's Buyer Protection Policy.
