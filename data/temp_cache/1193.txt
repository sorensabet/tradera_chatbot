QUESTION: What actions must a seller take to be eligible for Tradera's seller protection?
ANSWER: To be eligible for Tradera's seller protection, a seller must send the goods to the delivery address approved by Tradera at the time of payment. If the buyer's delivery address changes after purchase, the seller must send the goods to the new address. The seller must also provide proof of shipment for goods that are priced over 200 SEK and shipped with traceability. The seller must describe the goods in the advertisement and send them in an appropriate manner to minimize damage in transit.

QUESTION: What are the prohibited items for sale on Tradera?
ANSWER: Tradera prohibits the sale of tickets to events such as sports or concerts and the sale of goods and services that are not compliant with Tradera's Rules for Sellers.
