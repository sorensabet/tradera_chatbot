QUESTION: What is Tradera's buyer protection fee?
ANSWER: Tradera's buyer protection fee is 6% of the selling price with a minimum amount of 5 SEK and a maximum of 750 SEK, and it is included in the invoice fee.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/traderabetalning/handla-pa-faktura/

QUESTION: What happens if I delay payment on my invoice or installment payments?
ANSWER: If you delay the payment on your invoice or installment payments, you will be charged a reminder fee, default interest of 24%, and an administration fee of 29 SEK may be added if the payment is delayed even further. If the payment is delayed for a third time, the claim might be transferred to a dunning service where additional dunning fees may be added.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/traderabetalning/handla-pa-faktura/

QUESTION: How much is the reminder fee for late payments on Tradera?
ANSWER: The reminder fee for late payments on Tradera is 60 SEK, and a payment period of 10 days is added, along with 24% default interest.

QUESTION: How is the original invoice sent to the buyer?
