QUESTION: What is Bank ID?
\ANSWER: Bank ID is an electronic identification service used to verify the identity of a user on the internet. This service is used by banks, Försäkringskassan, Tradera and several other websites in Sweden. It is the biggest identification service in Sweden and is used to verify a user's identity before they can access certain services.
