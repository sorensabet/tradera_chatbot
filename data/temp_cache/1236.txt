QUESTION: What are the available payment methods when buying an item at Tradera?
ANSWER: The available payment methods when buying an item at Tradera are card payment, PayPal, ApplePay, and Google Pay. These payment methods are provided by third parties with whom Tradera cooperates for this purpose and are under the supervision of the Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority or its foreign counterparts. The payment methods are offered by these payment solution providers and they decide which payment methods they want to offer you.

QUESTION: What is the payout solution offered by Tradera?
ANSWER: Tradera offers a payout solution for sellers, which allows the seller to be paid through Tradera's payout solution in the event of a sale on Tradera's platform. However, the seller must comply with Tradera's terms and conditions and use its checkout solution when selling on Tradera's platform. The seller cannot claim payment in any way other than through the options available through Tradera's checkout solution.

QUESTION: Can the seller receive payment for the buyer's option of using Tradera's checkout solution?
ANSWER: No, the seller is not entitled to receive payment for the buyer's option of using Tradera's checkout solution. The seller can only claim payment through the options available through Tradera's checkout solution.
