QUESTION: Can I see all my payments in My Tradera?
ANSWER: Yes, you can see all your payments in My Tradera under the "Payments" tab. This tab includes information such as the date the payment processing began, the listing related to the payment, the type of payment, the buyer's alias, and the amount of the payment.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/hur-far-jag-betalt/

QUESTION: What happens if the buyer pays with invoice/installments?
ANSWER: If the buyer pays with invoice/installments, the money will be in your account in 1-2 banking days. For you as the seller, it works as if the buyer had paid for the listing with a credit card/PayPal.
