QUESTION 1: What is the purpose of Tradera's collaboration with the Police?
ANSWER: Tradera collaborates with the Police to provide assistance to users who have been victims of fraud or other crimes. They recommend users to make police reports in such incidents and provide them with as much information about the listing and the seller as possible. They also share information with the police to help them with their investigations.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/our-collaborations/
