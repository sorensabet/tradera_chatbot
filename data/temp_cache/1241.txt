QUESTION: What types of purchases are prohibited on Tradera's platform?
ANSWER: Tradera prohibits the purchase of real estate, vehicles (except for recreational vehicles, such as bicycles and hoverboards), customized objects, items equivalent to cash, event tickets, and goods and services that violate Tradera's Rules for Sellers.

QUESTION: How can disputes between buyers and sellers be resolved on Tradera's platform?
ANSWER: Disputes can be resolved through Tradera's dispute settlement procedure on "My Tradera". The dispute must be initiated within 30 days of the purchase, and any communication about the dispute must be made through Tradera's dispute settlement system on the platform. If the dispute is not resolved within ten days, the buyer can escalate it to Tradera. Tradera may ask both parties for supporting evidence to help resolve the dispute.

QUESTION: What happens if a buyer fails to escalate a dispute to Tradera within ten days?
ANSWER: If the buyer does not escalate the dispute to Tradera within ten days of initiating the dispute settlement procedure, the dispute settlement procedure will automatically be terminated in the seller's favor.
