QUESTION: How long is the right of withdrawal period?
ANSWER: The right of withdrawal period for a buyer on Tradera is 14 days from the day after they receive the item.

QUESTION: What law governs the right of withdrawal on Tradera?
ANSWER: The right of withdrawal on Tradera is governed by the law on distance contracts and contracts outside business premises (2005:59).

QUESTION: What is a business seller's obligation when it comes to the right of withdrawal on Tradera?
ANSWER: As a business seller on Tradera, one must offer and inform buyers about a 14-day right of withdrawal regardless of order value, according to the law on distance contracts and contracts outside business premises. Sellers are also required to inform buyers about the responsible party for the cost of a potential return shipping.

QUESTION: Who is responsible for the cost of a return shipping on Tradera?
ANSWER: It is the responsibility of the business seller on Tradera to inform the buyer before the purchase about who is responsible for the cost of a possible return shipping. If this is not clarified before the purchase is made, the seller is responsible for the cost of a return.
