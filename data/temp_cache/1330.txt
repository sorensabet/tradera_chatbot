QUESTION: What is the process for handling complaints at Tradera?
ANSWER: Tradera processes complaints swiftly and effectively through a system of three lines of support. If the dispute is not resolved by the second line support function, a third line support function will be involved to finally resolve it. The outcome of the complaint will be communicated to the user individually. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/

QUESTION: Will Tradera engage lawyers to resolve disputes with business users?
ANSWER: Yes, Tradera is willing to engage the lawyers Anders Arthur or Fredrik Norburg to attempt to reach an agreement with business users on the settlement of any disputes between Tradera and the business user arising in relation to the provision of the Services, including complaints that could not be resolved by means of Tradera's internal complaint-handling system. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2022-2023/
