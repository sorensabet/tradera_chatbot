QUESTION: Can stuffed animals and animal parts be sold on Tradera?
ANSWER: Stuffed animals, animal parts, and products listed in appendix A in the CITES list of endangered species and species protected according to EU's regulations on trade with wild animals and plants are not allowed to be sold on Tradera. However, if a CITES certificate from Jordbruksverket is included and specified in the listing, it is okay to sell these products. If the animal is in appendix B or C of the CITES list of endangered species, the seller must be able to prove that the animal or animal product was acquired legally. For more information, users can visit Jordbruksverket's website and the CITES organization.
