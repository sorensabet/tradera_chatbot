QUESTION: What is an integrated shipping method in Tradera?
ANSWER: An integrated shipping method in Tradera means that the tracking information will be automatically updated in the user's Mitt Tradera when the shipment is booked. For DB Schenker, it also means that the shipment can be booked directly through Tradera without visiting another site.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/foretag/

ANSWER: Using DB Schenker provides benefits such as no size restrictions, tracking information for both the buyer and seller, and insurance up to 5000 kr within Sweden. Members can also receive discounts up to 70% through Tradera.
