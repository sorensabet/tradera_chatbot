QUESTION 1: What are the obligations of a seller in relation to buyer protection? 
ANSWER: Sellers must fulfill their obligations in relation to Tradera's buyer's protection, regardless of the payment method by which the Buyer has chosen to pay. They must also promptly respond to any complaints from the buyer through Tradera's dispute settlement procedure and if they have already been paid, Tradera may then send an invoice to the seller for the amount of refund owed to the buyer. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 2: What is the extent of refunds that a seller is allowed to make without contacting Tradera's customer service? 
ANSWER: The seller may refund transactions up to 500 SEK without contacting Tradera's customer service. However, to refund transactions exceeding 500 SEK, the seller must first contact Tradera's customer service to receive instructions. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 3: What is buyer protection and when is it applicable? 
ANSWER: Under certain conditions, a Buyer has buyer protection which allows them to have disputes handled and to have the amount they have paid in a transaction refunded. The terms and conditions of Tradera's buyer protection and dispute settlement procedures are applicable in accordance with the prevailing terms and conditions for the buyer. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 4: Are refunds for transactions exceeding 500 SEK allowed without contacting Tradera's customer service? 
ANSWER: No, the seller must first contact Tradera's customer service to receive instructions in order to refund transactions exceeding 500 SEK. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 5: What happens if the seller does not promptly notify Tradera of the responses and supporting documents requested by Tradera? 
ANSWER: If the seller does not promptly notify Tradera of the responses and supporting documents requested by Tradera, Tradera may find that the buyer shall be entitled to a refund for a product without the seller having given his/her opinion. If the seller has already been paid, Tradera may then send an invoice to the seller for the amount of refund owed to the buyer, and the seller shall then be obliged to pay this amount to Tradera. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 6: When is it up to the seller to pay back part or the full amount to the buyer? 
ANSWER: If the Buyer is a consumer and the Seller is a trader, it is up to the Seller to pay back part or the full amount to the Buyer according to the statutory right of withdrawal. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 7: What is the maximum amount that the seller is obliged to refund to the buyer? 
ANSWER: The seller is obliged to refund the agreed amount to the buyer up to 500 SEK. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 8: Are all types of purchases covered by Tradera's buyer protection? 
ANSWER: No, certain types of purchases are never covered by Tradera's buyer protection. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 9: Can the seller make refunds through payment methods other than Tradera's payment solution? 
ANSWER: No, the seller must make refunds only through Tradera's payment solution, and not through other payment methods. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION 10: What happens if the seller and buyer agree to a return or price reduction? 
ANSWER: If the seller and buyer agree to a return or price reduction, this shall be promptly settled by the seller, who shall refund the agreed amount to the buyer up to 500 SEK. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/
