QUESTION: What are cookies?
ANSWER: Cookies are small data files that are stored in the memory of your browser or device when you visit a website, or read a message. They allow a website to recognize a particular device or browser. There are different types of cookies, such as session cookies and persistent cookies, and they can be disabled or removed by tools that are available in most browsers on the market.
