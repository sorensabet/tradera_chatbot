QUESTION: What are the payment methods available on Tradera?
ANSWER: The available payment methods on Tradera are Swish, Credit/Debit Card, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Paynova or PayPal. Paynova and PayPal always come with buyer protection that costs 5% of the total amount, minimum 5 SEK and max 750 SEK. For the other payment methods the buyer protection is optional except for Swish directly to the seller.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/shoppa-2/

QUESTION: How can I pay for a listing on Tradera?
ANSWER: You can pay for a listing on Tradera from My Tradera - My purchases. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/shoppa-2/

QUESTION: Is the buyer protection optional for all payment methods on Tradera?
ANSWER: Buyer protection is optional for all payment methods except for Paynova and PayPal which always come with buyer protection that costs 5% of the total amount, minimum 5 SEK and max 750 SEK. Swish directly to the seller also has mandatory buyer protection.
