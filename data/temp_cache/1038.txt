QUESTION: What is a buyer dispute on Tradera?
ANSWER: A buyer dispute on Tradera is a mediation process initiated by a buyer when a purchase has not gone as planned and the issue cannot be resolved with the seller. It involves submitting a short description of what went wrong and communicating with the seller before escalating the dispute to Tradera.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/tvist-kopare/

QUESTION: Who can initiate a dispute on Tradera?
ANSWER: Only buyers with buyer protection can initiate a dispute on Tradera.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/tvist-kopare/

QUESTION: What is Tradera's buyer protection?

QUESTION: How can a buyer escalate a dispute to Tradera?
ANSWER: A buyer can escalate a dispute to Tradera if they cannot find a common solution with the seller. They can do this by finding the listing on the "Your purchases" page, clicking on more, and choosing "Report missing item."
