QUESTION 1: What is the maximum number of identical listings that sellers are allowed to have on Tradera?
ANSWER: Sellers are allowed to have a maximum of five identical listings active, except for store listings which are limited to one identical listing since they have an inventory.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/limit-for-identical-items/

QUESTION 2: How does the restriction on the number of identical items help maintain fair competition and a good buyer experience?
ANSWER: The rule for limiting the number of identical items helps to prohibit one seller from monopolizing a category and spamming similar listings, therefore, maintaining fair competition and a good buyer experience.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/limit-for-identical-items/

QUESTION 3: Does the limit for identical items apply to all types of items?
ANSWER: The limit for identical items only applies to identical items. If you have several sizes of the same t-shirt, they can be listed as individual listings.
