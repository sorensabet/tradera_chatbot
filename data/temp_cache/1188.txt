QUESTION: How does the payment process work for sellers on the Tradera platform?
ANSWER: The payment process for sellers on the Tradera platform depends on the payment method chosen by the buyer. If the buyer chooses to pay via PayPal, Tradera verifies the transaction and, upon approval, PayPal pays the seller. If the buyer chooses to pay via Paynova or Klarna, the seller's right to be paid is transferred to PayPal or Tradera, respectively, and the seller is paid through Hyperwallet. Regardless of the payment method chosen, Tradera notifies the seller via email and in their My Tradera account that payment has been made and the seller should send the goods to the buyer.

The seller does not have to agree to payment by invoice or installment, but will still be paid in the same way as if the buyer had chosen to pay by card or PayPal. Tradera notifies the seller once payment has been made and the seller should then send the goods to the buyer.
