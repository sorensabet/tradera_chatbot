QUESTION: What is the purpose of a business account on Tradera?
ANSWER: The purpose of a business account on Tradera is to sell used and second-hand items as Tradera is primarily a circular marketplace that limits its store subscriptions to companies that sell used and second-hand goods. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/da/virksomhedskonto/

QUESTION: How many different sizes of store subscriptions are offered by Tradera?
ANSWER: Tradera offers four different sizes of store subscriptions named XS, S, M, and L, depending on the number of store ads. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/da/virksomhedskonto/

QUESTION: Does Tradera impose Swedish tax on the fees it charges?
ANSWER: No, all fees charged by Tradera are stated without Swedish VAT. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/da/virksomhedskonto/

QUESTION: What is the monthly fee for a Tradera store subscription without a binding period?
ANSWER: The monthly subscription fee for four different store sizes without a binding period ranges from SEK 295 to SEK 1845. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/da/virksomhedskonto/

QUESTION: What is the special provision fee for a Tradera store subscription?
ANSWER: The special provision fee for a Tradera store subscription is SEK 2.40 for all store sizes. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/da/virksomhedskonto/

QUESTION: Does Tradera allow stores to sell new items?
ANSWER: Yes, Tradera allows stores to sell new items, but they must specify in the ad whether the item is used or of collectible value and cannot be bought new today. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/da/virksomhedskonto/

QUESTION: What is the monthly fee for a special store subscription that sells collectibles?
ANSWER: The monthly fee for a special store subscription that sells collectibles ranges from SEK 155 to SEK 235, depending on the number of store ads and whether it has a binding period. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/da/virksomhedskonto/

QUESTION: What is the minimum store subscription size offered by Tradera?
ANSWER: The minimum store subscription size offered by Tradera is XS, which allows one to fifty store ads.

QUESTION: Is there a different commission rate for store ads that sell items only for less than 30 SEK?
ANSWER: Yes, there is a different commission rate for store ads that sell items only for less than 30 SEK, and it is 12% of the selling price.
