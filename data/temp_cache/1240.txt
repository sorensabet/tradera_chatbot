QUESTION: What is the maximum amount that a seller can refund without contacting Tradera's customer service?
ANSWER: The maximum amount a seller can refund without contacting Tradera's customer service is transactions that do not exceed 1000 SEK.

QUESTION: What is buyer protection?
ANSWER: In certain circumstances, buyer protection is an entitlement that allows a Buyer to have a dispute resolution process and be refunded when the buyer pays using Paynova, PayPal or Klarna, or pay with card, Google Pay, Apple Pay, or Swish and adds Tradera's buyer protection for a fee. The highest amount that can be refunded under buyer protection is 50,000 SEK.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-hw-1-4/

QUESTION: Are all purchases covered by Tradera's buyer protection?
ANSWER: No, certain types of purchases are never covered by Tradera's buyer protection such as purchase of properties (including shares in property websites), vehicles (including accessories) and travel tickets.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-hw-1-4/

QUESTION: Can the seller refund the buyer using payment methods other than Tradera's checkout solution?
ANSWER: No, the seller must make refunds only through Tradera's checkout solution and is not allowed to refund through other payment methods.

QUESTION: Are buyers charged for Tradera's buyer protection?
ANSWER: When paying with card, Google Pay, Apple Pay, or Swish, the Buyer can add Tradera's buyer protection for a fee. It is stated on Tradera's platform before the Buyer chooses the payment method whether buyer protection is included or can be purchased.

QUESTION: Is it the seller's responsibility to refund the buyer?
ANSWER: Yes, if a transaction is to be fully or partly repaid by the seller on the basis of an agreement between the seller and the buyer, it is up to the seller to pay back part or the full amount to the buyer promptly.
