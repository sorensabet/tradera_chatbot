QUESTION: What is a refundable amount on Tradera's Platform?
ANSWER: "Refundable amount" is the amount that the Seller will refund to Tradera in connection with a refund of an amount already paid to the Seller as a result of a purchase being canceled. The amount is part or all of the Transaction, plus any fees charged to Tradera in connection with the Transaction, including shipping charges for the return of a product, and charges imposed on Tradera for a transaction in relation to its partners such as PayPal, Paynova, VISA, or MasterCard.

QUESTION: How does Tradera's payment solutions work?
ANSWER: Tradera's payment solution means that the Buyer may use different means of payment for the goods.
