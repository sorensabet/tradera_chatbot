QUESTION: What is the purpose of the store subscription limitations?
ANSWER: The store subscription limitations are there to limit store subscriptions to businesses that sell used and second-hand products as Tradera is primarily a circular marketplace. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/foretag/

QUESTION: How are the settings that apply to your store and the publication of store listings handled?
ANSWER: The settings that apply to your store and the publication of store listings are handled through Sello.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/foretag/

QUESTION: What are the benefits of having a store in Tradera? 
ANSWER: Having a store in Tradera provides benefits such as having your alias be searchable in the search field on Tradera's home page, the opportunity to add more information about you as a seller and your company in the tabs for conditions of sales and contact information, and more control over settings that apply to your store and the publication of store listings via Sello.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/foretag/
