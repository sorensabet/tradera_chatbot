QUESTION: How long does a seller have to wait for payment on Tradera?
ANSWER: As a general rule, the seller is paid within two (2) business days on Tradera.

QUESTION: Can Tradera withhold payment from a seller?
ANSWER: Yes, Tradera reserves the right to withhold payment if there is a suspicion that the Seller will not fulfill their obligations.

QUESTION: How does a swish payment work on Tradera?
ANSWER: When a buyer wishes to pay by Swish on Tradera, the payment goes directly from the buyer's account to the seller's account. Tradera's payment solution facilitates the transaction by automatically filling in the seller's telephone number in the Swish app, and the buyer can then authorize the payment directly.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION: What happens when a buyer pays by card or PayPal on Tradera?
ANSWER: If the buyer chooses to pay by card or by PayPal on Tradera, the money will first be deposited in PayPal's (Hyperwallet) bank account. Tradera will then verify the transaction and, if it is approved by Tradera, PayPal will then pay the money to the seller. Once the buyer has paid, Tradera will notify the seller via e-mail and in My Tradera that payment has been made. The goods should then be sent by the seller to the buyer.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION: How does payment by invoice or installments work on Tradera?
ANSWER: If the buyer chooses to pay by invoice or installment on Tradera, the buyer enters into an agreement with Paynova on Tradera. It is not the seller who has to agree to payment by invoice or installment, but the seller is paid in the same way as if the buyer had chosen to pay by card or PayPal. After Paynova carries out a normal credit check of the buyer, money will be paid to PayPal (Hyperwallet). Once Tradera has carried out its check of the transaction and it has been approved, the seller will be notified. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-2020-2022/

QUESTION: Who does the buyer enter into an agreement with for payment by invoice or installments on Tradera?
ANSWER: The buyer enters into an agreement with Paynova on Tradera for payment by invoice or installment.
