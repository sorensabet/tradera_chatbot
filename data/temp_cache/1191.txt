QUESTION: How can a dispute between a Seller and a Buyer be resolved on Tradera's platform?
ANSWER: Tradera offers a dispute settlement system on "My Tradera". If a dispute arises between a Seller and a Buyer, they may resolve it by initiating a dispute settlement procedure on "My Tradera", communicating through Tradera's dispute settlement system on the platform, and trying to find a solution to the problem. If they fail to reach a resolution within ten (10) days, the Buyer may escalate the dispute to Tradera.
