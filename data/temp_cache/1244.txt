QUESTION: What does Tradera recommend the seller to do when sending goods to buyers?
ANSWER: Tradera recommends the Seller to send the goods with traceability and choose a mode of transport in which the goods sold are insured during transport up to the value of the goods. The Seller should also describe the goods in a satisfactory manner in the advertisement and deliver the goods to the carrier in the same condition as described in the advertisement. The Seller should have sent the goods to the Buyer in an appropriate manner; i.e., delivery should take place with all ordered goods being packaged and shipped in a manner that minimizes the risk of damage to the products.
