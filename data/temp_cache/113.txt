QUESTION: How must messages be sent according to Tradera's user agreement?
ANSWER: Messages pursuant to this User Agreement must be sent by registered post to Tradera Sweden AB, Box 45026, 104 30 Stockholm. We will send messages to you by e-mail to the e-mail address you have registered with Tradera.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2019-2020/

QUESTION: What should a user do if they receive a message stating that the e-mail address is not valid?
ANSWER: If the party sending the e-mail receives a message stating that the e-mail address is not valid, the e-mail will not be considered received by the user.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2019-2020/

QUESTION: How long does it take for Tradera to consider a message received if sent by registered post?
ANSWER: If messages sent by registered post will be considered to have been received by the receiving party three days after the message was sent.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2019-2020/

QUESTION: How long after an e-mail was sent would it be considered received if no message is received stating that the e-mail address is invalid?
ANSWER: You will be considered to have received the message 24 hours after the e-mail was sent if the party sending the e-mail does not receive a message stating that the e-mail address is not valid.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/anvandaravtal-eng-2019-2020/
