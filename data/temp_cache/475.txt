QUESTION: What is Net Payout at Tradera?
ANSWER: Net payouts is a system implemented by Tradera to make it easier for sellers where fees will be charged before making the payout to the seller's account, rather than being invoiced at the end of the month.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/nettoutbetalning/

QUESTION: What are the payment options that don't qualify for Net payouts?
ANSWER: Net payouts work for all payment options except for Swish.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/nettoutbetalning/

QUESTION: How does Tradera initiate a payout for sellers using Net Payouts?
ANSWER: If a seller's account balance is positive at the start of a weekday, Tradera will initiate a payout.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/nettoutbetalning/

QUESTION: What happens if a seller has a negative balance at the end of the month using Net Payouts?
ANSWER: If a seller has a negative balance by the end of the month, Tradera will invoice the remainder.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/nettoutbetalning/

QUESTION: What events are shown on the Payments & Fees page in descending order?
ANSWER: All events on the Payments & Fees page are shown in descending order with the latest event at the top.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/nettoutbetalning/
