QUESTION: How can disputes be resolved between Seller and Buyer on Tradera? 
ANSWER: Disputes can be resolved between Seller and Buyer on Tradera by means of the dispute settlement system offered by Tradera on "My Tradera". Such a dispute settlement procedure must be initiated in My Tradera within 30 days from the date of the purchase. Once a dispute settlement procedure has been initiated, Buyer and Seller must first communicate with each other through Tradera's dispute settlement system to try to find a solution to the problem. If Buyer and Seller fail to reach a resolution within ten (10) days of the initiation of the dispute settlement procedure, the Buyer may escalate the dispute to Tradera.
