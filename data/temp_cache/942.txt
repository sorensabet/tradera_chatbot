QUESTION: What should be done in case of a problem with a purchase made with Klarna?
ANSWER: The first step is to contact the seller. In case no agreement can be reached with the seller, you can start a dispute to use the buyer protection. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/klarna/

QUESTION: Do I need to pay even if I win the dispute after using buyer protection in Klarna?
ANSWER: Yes, you still have to pay for the buyer protection premium because, it is similar to an insurance policy. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/klarna/

QUESTION: As a seller, what actions should I take to enable my buyers to use Klarna?
ANSWER: Nothing needs to be done as Klarna is automatically added as a payment option for all Swedish buyers and active listings will automatically have the option to use Klarna.
