QUESTION: What happens if the Seller has not provided accurate payment information?
ANSWER: If the Seller has not provided accurate payment information, Tradera will not be able to pay the Seller. In such case, Tradera will contact the Seller to request the outstanding information. Once Tradera has received the information required to make the payment, the Seller will, as a general rule, be paid within two (2) business days.

QUESTION: Can Tradera withhold payment from a Seller?
ANSWER: Yes, Tradera can withhold payment if and for as long as there is a suspicion that the Seller will not fulfill his/her obligations.

QUESTION: What is the process for paying with a card or PayPal or Swish on Tradera?
ANSWER: If a Buyer chooses to pay by card or by PayPal or Swish, the money will first be deposited in Tradera's bank account. Tradera will then verify the Transaction and, if it is approved by Tradera, Tradera will then pay the money to the Seller. Once the Buyer has paid, Tradera will notify the Seller via e-mail and in My Tradera that payment has been made. The goods should then be sent by the Seller to the Buyer.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng-1-4/

QUESTION: How does the payment process work via Paynova?
ANSWER: If the Buyer has chosen to pay via Paynova, the Seller accepts that PayPal takes over the Seller's right to be paid by the Buyer in exchange for PayPal to pay Tradera, who, through Tradera's payment service, will pay the Seller.
