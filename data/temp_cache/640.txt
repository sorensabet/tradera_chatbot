QUESTION: Why is ending a listing by finishing the deal with the seller outside of Tradera prohibited?
ANSWER: Ending a listing by finishing the deal with the seller outside of Tradera is prohibited to safeguard the interests of both parties in case of a dispute as when a deal is closed at Tradera, they have the possibility to help both parties. Also, most listings at Tradera have the option to pay with their integrated payment services which adds a layer of security for the buyer.
