QUESTION: What is Tradera’s cooperation with rights owners?
ANSWER: Tradera’s cooperation with rights owners means that as a rights owner you can work together with the company to keep their site free of objects and content that infringe on your rights. This cooperation can benefit both parties by helping to protect brands and mutual buyers from buying items that may be counterfeits or prohibited. 
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/traderas-cooperation-rights-owners/

QUESTION: What benefits can a rights owner gain from cooperating with Tradera?
ANSWER: Cooperating with Tradera can help a rights owner protect their brand, prioritize and handle reported items within a shorter period of time, and protect mutual buyers from purchasing counterfeit or prohibited items.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/traderas-cooperation-rights-owners/

QUESTION: How can a rights owner get started with reporting infringement on Tradera?
ANSWER: To get started with reporting infringement on Tradera, a rights owner should fill in the details on the form and submit a Notice of Infringement-document. They can email the document to vero@tradera.com, and after the initial submission, they only need to email the item number and type of infringement to Tradera.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/traderas-cooperation-rights-owners/

QUESTION: What is the purpose of a NOCI-document?
ANSWER: The Notice of Infringement document (NOCI-document) is designed to ensure that Tradera has all the necessary information to identify and remove infringing items from their platform.
