QUESTION: How does Tradera's buyer protection work?
ANSWER: Tradera's buyer protection provides protection to buyers in case they have not received the delivery of a paid item or if the delivery of the goods is substantially different from the seller's description. The Seller must fulfill his/her obligations to Tradera's buyer protection, irrespective of the payment method by which the buyer has chosen to pay. However, certain types of purchases are not covered by Tradera's buyer protection.
URL: https://info.tradera.com/en/security-center/betalningsvillkor-eng/

QUESTION:Which type of purchases are excluded from Tradera's buyer protection?
ANSWER: Tradera's buyer protection does not cover certain types of purchases that include the purchase of properties, customized objects, tickets for events, etc.

QUESTION: How can a Seller and Buyer resolve their differences in case of disputes?
ANSWER: In case of a difference of opinion between a Seller and a Buyer, they can resolve their differences using the dispute settlement system offered by Tradera on "My Tradera". The dispute settlement procedure must be initiated within thirty (30) days from the date of purchase, and all communication regarding the dispute should go through Tradera.

QUESTION: In what circumstances can a Buyer be entitled to a refund from a Seller?
ANSWER: If a Seller does not promptly notify Tradera of the responses and supporting documents requested by Tradera, Tradera may find that the Buyer shall be entitled to a refund for a product without the Seller having given his/her opinion. If the Seller has already been paid, Tradera may then send an invoice to the Seller for the amount of refund owed to the Buyer, and the Seller shall then be obliged to pay this amount to Tradera.

QUESTION: Can a Buyer purchase tickets for events on Tradera?
ANSWER: No, the purchase of tickets for events, such as sports or concert tickets, is not covered by Tradera's buyer protection.

QUESTION: What kind of purchases are prohibited by Tradera's Rules for Sellers?
ANSWER: Tradera's Rules for Sellers prohibits certain types of purchases that include the purchase of goods and services that violate the law or regulation, infringe the rights of third parties, or are contrary to good morals.

QUESTION: Is the purchase of vehicles covered under Tradera's buyer protection?
ANSWER: No, except for private portable light vehicles used for recreational purposes such as bicycles and wheeled hoverboards, the purchase of vehicles including motor vehicles, motorcycles, caravans, aircraft, and boats, is not covered under Tradera's buyer protection.

QUESTION: Do recipients qualify for Tradera's buyer protection when purchasing customized objects?
ANSWER: No, the purchase of customized objects is not covered under Tradera's buyer protection unless the recipient claims that the objects have not arrived.
