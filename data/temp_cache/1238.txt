QUESTION: What happens if the Seller has not provided accurate payment information?
ANSWER: In case the Seller has not provided accurate payment information, Tradera will not be able to pay the Seller and will contact them to request the necessary information. Once the Seller provides the necessary information, they will typically be paid within two business days.

QUESTION: What is Tradera's policy regarding payment to a Seller if there is suspicion that the Seller will not fulfill their obligations?
ANSWER: Tradera reserves the right to withhold payment if there is a suspicion that the Seller will not fulfill their obligations.

QUESTION: Can a Seller contact Tradera on various matters related to a transaction?

QUESTION: What is the Seller's responsibility upon confirmation of payment from the Buyer?

QUESTION: What happens if a Swedish Buyer chooses to pay by invoice or installment?
ANSWER: If a Swedish Buyer chooses to pay by invoice or installment, they enter into an agreement with Paynova or Klarna, and it is not the Seller who has to agree to payment by invoice or installment.

QUESTION: If a Swedish Buyer chooses to pay via Paynova, who takes over the Seller's right to be paid by the Swedish Buyer?
